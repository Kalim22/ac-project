Central DX Systems

HVAC Central DX Systems (Direct Expansion Systems) are an air conditioning system that uses an outdoor condenser to cool refrigerant which is then sent to a fan coil unit in the building.

Air Handling Unit (AHU)

HVAC (Heating, Ventilation and Air Conditioning) AHU (Air Handling Unit) is a device used to regulate and circulate air as part of a heating, ventilating, and air-conditioning (HVAC) system.

VRF/VRV system

HVAC VRV (variable refrigerant volume) is a type of air conditioning system that uses variable speed compressors and thermostatically controlled valves to provide individual zones of cooling and/or heating.

Car parking ventilation

HVAC Car Parking Ventilation is a ventilation system designed to provide air circulation and air quality control in parking building. It is typically comprised of fans, ducts, and vents, and is designed to draw in fresh air from outside the building and expel stale air from the building.

Insulated-Air-Ducting

HVAC Insulated-Air-Ducting is a type of ductwork used in HVAC (heating, ventilation, and air conditioning) systems. It is designed to reduce heat loss through the ducts and provide a more efficient and comfortable environment in the building.

Variable Air Volume(VAV)
